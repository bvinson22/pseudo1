What's Happening?

  • :hover - Move your mouse over any link to see it slide right
  • :active - Click and hold ANY link to see it turn dark red
  • :first-child - Notice how "Mission Control" has a special green color
  • :last-child - "Deep Space" has a unique purple color
  • :nth-child(2) - "Launch Pad" has a yellow color

Important: To make sure the active state works on all items, including those with special colors, add !important to your active state like this: background-color: #your-color !important;
